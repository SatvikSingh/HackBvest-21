About

Flagship Event

HackBVEST’21 is a hackathon being organized by Bharati Vidyapeeth’s College of Engineering as the flagship event of its annual tech fest BVEST.

Global Innovation

“Innovation is the calling card of the future and young people should be at the forefront of global change.” Keeping this in mind, the primary theme for this year is TOWARDS GLOBAL INNOVATION.

24 Hours of Brainstorming!

It will be a 24-hour Hackathon with various mentoring sessions in between, to ensure that teams get the best guidance in the sectors of technology and entrepreneurship, for their respective projects.
